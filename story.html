首要原理Foundational Principle
间隔效应与检索练习Spacing & Retrieval Practice
认知科学中最有实证支持的记忆巩固方式之一 One of the most empirically supported memory consolidation strategies in cognitive science
○ 主动检索，胜于重复阅读 Active retrieval beats re-reading ▼
主动回想词语（recall）比反复翻阅词表更能建立持久记忆。每一次答题，都是一次主动提取，而非被动接收。 Actively recalling a word strengthens memory far more than passively reviewing it. Every question answered is an act of retrieval, not passive exposure.
○ 分散练习，胜于集中突击 Spaced practice beats massed study ▼
将词汇练习分散在多个时段，比临考前集中背诵效果更持久。自主学习平台让学生可以随时练习，自然形成间隔效应。 Distributing vocabulary practice across multiple sessions produces longer-lasting retention than cramming. An SDL platform makes this natural — students practise whenever they choose, spacing automatically.
○ 词王淬炼坊如何落实这一原理 How 词王淬炼坊 applies this ▼
课前预习词汇 → 课堂接触课文语境 → 课后平台反复检索巩固。每一次接触都是一次间隔后的主动提取，这正是记忆巩固最有效的方式。 Pre-class: students preview vocabulary independently. In class: words appear in textbook context. After class: platform retrieval consolidates retention. Each encounter is a spaced retrieval event — exactly how memory consolidates most effectively.
Karpicke, J. D., & Roediger, H. L. (2007). Expanding retrieval practice promotes short-term retention, but equally spaced retrieval enhances long-term retention. Journal of Experimental Psychology: Learning, Memory, and Cognition, 33(4), 704–719. → Full paper
RAT 框架 · 技术整合层级RAT Framework · Technology Integration Level
R
替代Replacement
将现有教学方式移至数字平台，学习任务与成效不变。Moving existing teaching methods to a digital format, with no functional changes to the task and students' learning.
学生的学习成果不变，只是记录在数字平台上。Impact: My students' learning is the same, except their artefacts are recorded on the digital platform.
A
扩增Amplification
通过技术改进现有教学方式，提升学习任务质量与成效。Improving/refining existing teaching methods through the use of technology, with some enhancements to the task and students' learning.
学生学习得到提升，例如能在需要时获得即时反馈，对学习有更大的自主性。Impact: My students' learning is enhanced e.g. they receive and understand the feedback when they need it and have greater agency in their learning.
T
革新Transformation
以传统教学法根本无法实现的方式开展教与学，学习任务与成效发生根本性改变。Teaching and learning in ways that would not be possible without technology, with key changes to the task and students' learning.
学生的学习得到根本性转化，例如在完成任务的过程中同步获得对自身学习历程的反馈。Impact: My students' learning is transformed e.g. they receive feedback on their learning process while completing the task.
词王淬炼坊定位于 革新（Transformation） 层级：实时掌握度追踪、跨设备进度同步、个人化薄弱词汇地图，均为传统词汇练习所无法实现的学习体验。 词王淬炼坊 operates at the Transformation level: real-time mastery tracking, cross-device progress sync, and personalised weak-word mapping are learning experiences not achievable through traditional vocabulary practice.

激励Motivation
KAT 6 · 自我决定理论驱动内在动力KAT 6 · Self-determination theory drives intrinsic motivation
Deci 与 Ryan 的自我决定理论指出，学习动力来自三种基本心理需求：能力感（掌握词汇获得 XP 与徽章）、自主感（学生自选学习单元与模式）、归属感（排行榜与班级社群）。积分系统有明确的学习逻辑，答对越早奖励越高，鼓励扎实掌握而非碰运气。 Deci & Ryan's SDT identifies three basic needs driving intrinsic motivation: competence (XP and badges for mastering vocab), autonomy (students choose their own units and modes), and relatedness (leaderboard community). The scoring system rewards earlier mastery more, incentivising genuine learning.
Deci, E. L., & Ryan, R. M. (2000). The "what" and "why" of goal pursuits. Psychological Inquiry, 11(4), 227–268.

KAT 7 · 学生主导学习节奏与路径KAT 7 · Students control their own pace and path

They can switch to unmastered vocab review at any time. The platform provides structure and support; the learning path is the student's own.

第一阶段 · 词库建设Stage 1 · Vocabulary Foundation

▼
背景与判断Context & Rationale
每册教材的附录均收录《生字词总表》，是学生词汇学习的官方参考。然而，这份词表仅列出词语本身，没有释义、词性标注、英文对译，也没有文化站词汇。建立这个平台，首先意味着在此基础上，建立一份更具教学实用性的词汇资源库。 Each textbook volume includes a 生字词总表 as an appendix — the official vocabulary reference. However, this list contains only the words, with no definitions, part-of-speech labels, English translations, or Cultural Corner coverage. Building this platform meant constructing a more pedagogically complete resource on this foundation.
📚
课文例句全程溯源Every Sentence Traced to Source
全部词汇的课文例句均回溯至原始教材，标注来源（原文直引 / 改编），确保语料权威性。Every example sentence traced to the original e-textbook, tagged as verbatim or adapted.
🏛️
文化站：77 个附录未收录的词汇Cultural Corner: 77 Items the Appendix Missed
逐一提取 23 个文化站单元，补入 77 个词汇，中一至中四全覆盖。这是本平台独有的词库深度。All 23 Cultural Corner units across Sec 1–4 extracted manually — 77 items found in no other comparable resource.
🏷️
词性标注规范Part-of-Speech Schema
约 20 个标签，谚语 / 俗语 / 成语严格区分；双词性按动词优先排列。~20 canonical labels. 谚语, 俗语, 成语 carefully distinguished. Verb-first for dual-class entries.
✏️
填空句另行撰写Separate Cloze Sentences
为每个词另写填空句，检测词汇在新语境中的实际运用，而非句子熟悉度。A separate cloze sentence for every word — testing application in new contexts, not sentence familiarity.
遇到的问题 → 点击查看解决方案Problems encountered → tap to see solutions
例句太容易认出来Sentences too recognisable
例句需要人工全面审核Sentences needed thorough human review
文化站词汇无从参照No reference for Cultural Corner vocab
问题Problem
闪卡与填空用同一例句，学生可根据句子熟悉度猜答案，绕过真实词汇掌握的检验。Shared sentences allowed students to answer from sentence memory, bypassing genuine word knowledge.
方案Solution
为每个词另行撰写填空句，语境不同、句型不同，专门测试词汇在新语境中的迁移运用。A completely separate cloze sentence authored for every word — different context and structure — to test genuine transfer.
问题Problem
AI 辅助审查例句时，部分建议在语义或语用上存在偏差，不能直接采用。AI-assisted review produced some suggestions with semantic or pragmatic inaccuracies that could not be used directly.
方案Solution
经科组同事细心逐句审阅并提供宝贵反馈，对全部例句进行了系统性人工核查与修订。教师判断始终是最终标准。A colleague's careful sentence-by-sentence review and invaluable feedback enabled a systematic human audit and revision of all example sentences. Teacher judgement remained the final standard throughout.
问题Problem
教材附录完全没有文化站词汇，没有任何现成参照列表。The textbook appendix contained no Cultural Corner vocabulary and no existing reference list to draw from.
方案Solution
逐课翻阅中一至中四电子教材，手工提取全部 23 个文化站单元，并按同等标准进行标注。Every e-textbook from Sec 1–4 was manually reviewed. All 23 Cultural Corner units extracted and annotated to the same standard as the main vocabulary bank.

第二阶段 · 学习模式设计Stage 2 · Learning Mode Design

教学逻辑Pedagogical Logic
WA 和 EOY 的词汇专项占分有限，但词汇积累渗透进阅读理解、作文表达、聆听、口试的每一个环节。四种学习模式正是为了在日常自主练习中，系统性地建立这种积累。 While dedicated vocabulary items in WA and EOY carry limited direct weightage, vocabulary depth shapes performance across comprehension, composition, oral, and listening. The four modes build this depth through daily self-directed practice.
🃏
词语闪卡Flashcard Mode
认词与自评。翻牌后自主判断"已掌握 / 还需练习"，培养自我监控意识。Recognition + self-assessment. Students judge their own mastery after each flip, building self-monitoring awareness.
📝
填空挑战Cloze Challenge
语境填空，2×2 选项格。独立例句，专检词汇在语境中的实际运用。Context-based cloze with 2×2 option grid. Separate sentences test genuine contextual application.
🔍
华文解释Chinese Definition Mode
由中文释义反查词语，训练词义理解的深度与精准度。Identify words from Chinese definitions — developing precise semantic comprehension.
🌐
英文翻译English Translation Mode
由英文释义反查词语，为英语主导的学生建立双语理解桥梁。Identify words from English definitions — a cross-linguistic bridge for English-dominant students.
遇到的问题 → 点击查看解决方案Problems encountered → tap to see solutions
题序可被预判Question order predictable
闪卡翻牌故障Flashcard flip malfunction
语音功能受设备限制Voice function limited by device
问题Problem
学生发现填空题顺序与闪卡一致，可以根据顺序推断答案，降低了测试的有效性。Students noticed the cloze question order mirrored flashcards and used it to guess, undermining assessment validity.
方案Solution
切换模式后即时重新洗牌，每次练习呈现不同顺序，有效排除顺序记忆干扰。Questions reshuffled on every mode switch, eliminating order-based guessing entirely.
问题Problem
闪卡翻转动画在不同设备上出现错位或卡死，影响学习体验。The flashcard flip animation displayed incorrectly on certain devices — cards stuck or misaligned.
方案Solution
将共享 CSS class 的设计完全重构为两个独立 div，翻转逻辑用 JavaScript 直接控制，彻底消除继承冲突。Rebuilt as two fully independent divs with JavaScript-controlled display switching, eliminating CSS inheritance conflicts.
问题Problem
语音朗读练习是自然的扩展方向，但 iOS 完全不支持 Web Speech API。Voice pronunciation practice was a natural extension, but iOS does not support Web Speech API at all.
方案Solution
暂不纳入现阶段范围。功能的边界与平台的稳定性同样重要——知道在哪里停止，是判断力的一部分。Deferred from the current scope. Knowing where to stop is as important as knowing what to build.

第三阶段 · 游戏化系统Stage 3 · Gamification System
让持续练习有意义Making Sustained Practice Meaningful
每一个奖励机制，都直接绑定词汇学习的真实里程碑。Every reward is anchored to a genuine vocabulary learning milestone.
▼
设计原则Design Principle
积分系统有明确的学习逻辑：第一次答对 10 分，第二次 5 分，第三次及以后 2 分；掌握整个板块额外 +20 分。答对越早奖励越高，直接鼓励扎实掌握，而非反复碰运气。这与自我决定理论中"能力感"的激励机制直接对应。Clear pedagogical logic: 10 points first-attempt correct, 5 for second, 2 thereafter, plus 20-point component mastery bonus. Higher rewards for earlier mastery incentivise genuine learning — directly mapping to the competence need in Self-Determination Theory.

⭐→✨→🏅→👑，对应板块、单元、年级、全部完成。最高级引用：锲而不舍，金石可镂。⭐→✨→🏅→👑 across component, unit, year-level, full completion. Ultimate tier: 锲而不舍，金石可镂.

问题Problem
学生依赖手动复制进度码，丢失或换设备，数周练习积累清零。Students relied on manually copying a progress code. Losing it or switching devices wiped all accumulated progress.
方案Solution
全面迁移至 Firebase Auth + Firestore，凭邮箱登录，进度自动云端同步。Full migration to Firebase Auth + Firestore. Email login; progress auto-syncs to the cloud from any device.
问题Problem
三星 Android 设备上 TTS 完全无声，其他设备正常。TTS produced complete silence on Samsung Android devices while working on all others.
方案Solution
诊断发现 getVoices() 在三星设备同步返回空数组，用 onvoiceschanged + 200ms 重试机制修复。Diagnosed: getVoices() returns empty array synchronously on Samsung. Fixed with onvoiceschanged listener + 200ms retry.
问题Problem
同一设备切换账号时，前一个账号的本地进度被后一个账号继承，造成进度错误。Switching accounts on the same device caused the previous account's local progress to bleed into the new account.
方案Solution
登录时检测邮箱是否与本地记录一致，若不符则自动清除本地数据，确保每个账号进度完全独立。On login, incoming email is checked against the locally stored email. Mismatch triggers a local data wipe before loading.

G1 / G2 平台的差异化构想A Different Vision for G1 / G2
G1 与 G2 学生的自主动力基础较弱，更需要系统性地照顾自我决定理论（SDT）中的三种基本心理需求——能力感、自主感与归属感——才能建立持续学习的内在动力。这意味着平台的设计思路必须根本不同：

每日打卡与连续激励 · 建立学习习惯的持续动力
冒险叙事与角色扮演 · 以剧情驱动词语学习的探索感
口语发音测试 · 遇到生词即时练习发音，与国家考试接轨
打字技能整合 · 输入练习呼应华文打字考试要求

这需要的不只是技术，而是游戏设计、教学设计与学生心理研究的深度跨界合作。 G1 and G2 students have a weaker foundation of intrinsic motivation, requiring more deliberate scaffolding of all three needs identified in Self-Determination Theory (SDT) — competence, autonomy, and relatedness — to build sustained engagement. This means the platform design must be fundamentally different:

Daily check-ins & streak motivation · Building consistent learning habits
Adventure narrative & avatar roleplay · Story-driven vocabulary exploration
Oral pronunciation testing · Immediate pronunciation practice aligned with national exam requirements
Typing skill integration · Input practice aligned with Chinese typing exam components

This requires not just technology, but deep cross-disciplinary collaboration across game design, instructional design, and student motivation research.
